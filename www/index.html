The eMetabolomics research project

The eMetabolomics project is funded by the Netherlands eScience Center, in collaboration with Wageningen University and the Netherlands Metabolomics Centre. The project develops chemo-informatics based methods for metabolite identification and biochemical network reconstruction in an integrative metabolomics data analysis workflow.

Current status

The first efforts in this research project are directed at the automatic annotation of complex multistage MSn spectra from metabolomics experiments.
